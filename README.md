## Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive analysis of Maven Toys’ business performance from January 2022 to September 2023. The analysis focuses on sales trends, geographic distribution, and inventory management to identify key profitability drivers and uncover operational improvement opportunities, particularly in stock optimization.

The goal of this project is to support data-driven decision-making by aligning financial performance with inventory control and store-level demand.

## Analysis Report
Between January 2022 and September 2023, the business maintained a strong
financial position, generating $4.01M in Total Gross Profit with a healthy 27.79%
margin. While sales fluctuated, reaching a low point in August 2022, the company
successfully rebounded and remained profitable throughout the period.

Ciudad de Mexico is the top-performing city. From a location standpoint, Downtown stores generate the highest sales. This is largely due to footprint where
there are 29 Downtown locations, significantly outperforming Commercial with 12, Residential with 6, and Airport with only 3 locations.

While many stores face out-of-stock issues, Campeche and Chetumal are the
only cities that currently have no "Out of Stock" products. In terms of available
inventory,the Deck of Cards represents the highest inventory investment, leading
in both stock-on-hand 2,738 units and total inventory cost but it is also in the top 4
Total Units sold products, suggesting a need to balance high-cost stock levels
with the replenishment of frequently sold-out items.

Moving forward, the business should reallocate our budget to prioritize restocking
based on localized demand ensuring that our most popular products are always
available in our most profitable locations to minimize lost sales and maximize cash
flow.
